Our Mission

Our mission is to change the way that people discover new job opportunities. We were born from the belief that job seeking industry needs innovation, even after decades people still create boring job descriptions and boring resumes. People are spending so much time searching for jobs based on keywords and tons of filters that it discourages them from looking entirely. With our unique skill and strength based job matching algorithm, you can find a job in half the time!

Team Overview

We're a group of developers, designers, mathematicians, data scientists and researchers that work relentlessly to analyze jobs, skills and users strength to connect user to a job which is skill fit and culture fit.
